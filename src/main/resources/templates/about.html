About Us

Meet our Founder - Alex Carter

Alex Carter grew up in a small town where books were his world. He loved literature at an early age, inspired by authors like J. K. Rowling, J.R.R. Tolkien, and Tom Clancy. After studying English Literature, he created BookClub, a space where readers from across the globe could gather and express their passion for reading.

With BookClub, Alex has built a community where members read books, interview authors, and share book recommendations. His mission is simple: to make reading a social and rewarding experience.
